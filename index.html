✦ FAQs

Frequently Asked
Questions

What exactly is an AI avatar?

+

An AI avatar is a hyper-realistic digital version of you — your face, your voice, your expressions. Once we create it, it can deliver any video script without you ever sitting in front of a camera again. Your patients won't be able to tell the difference.

Which tools do you use to create my avatar and videos?

+

We use a combination of the latest AI video generation tools including Kling AI, Google Veo 3, and other cutting-edge platforms depending on what produces the best quality output for your specific avatar. We don't lock ourselves into one tool — we use whatever gives you the most realistic and professional result.

How do you write my scripts?

+

We don't guess. First we study your competitors, analyse the highest performing content in your medical niche, and understand your goals and target patients. Then we curate scripts specifically for you. Every script is shared with you for review and discussion — we only move to production once you are 100% happy with it. You can also share scripts or references you like and we build around them.

Do I need to record myself every month?

+

No. You record once during onboarding — a simple 15-minute session. That's it. After that your AI avatar handles every single video. You never need to sit in front of a camera again.

Will my patients know it's an AI avatar?

+

The quality of today's AI avatars is incredibly realistic. Most viewers cannot tell the difference. That said, we always recommend being authentic with your audience if asked — it actually builds more trust when doctors are transparent about using smart technology to share their expertise at scale.

How long does it take to get started?

+

From your first call to your first video going live is typically 10 to 14 days. Week one is discovery, competitor research, avatar creation and script curation. Week two is production and approval. From month two onwards everything runs like clockwork every month.

Which platforms do you post on?

+

We post across Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn and Facebook. Every video is formatted natively for each platform — correct aspect ratio, captions, hashtags and posting time optimised for maximum reach.

How many videos do I get every month?

+

Our standard package includes 20 videos every month. This covers Reels, Shorts and talking head content across all platforms. Custom packages with higher volumes are also available depending on your goals.

What kind of doctors do you work with?

+

We work exclusively with healthcare professionals — dermatologists, dentists, surgeons, gynaecologists, orthopaedic doctors, ayurveda practitioners, clinic owners and more. If you are a doctor who wants to build a personal brand online, we are built for you.

What if I don't like the content?

+

Nothing goes live without your approval. Every script is discussed with you first. If you ever feel a video doesn't represent you well we revise it — no questions asked. Your brand is our responsibility.

Is there a lock-in contract?

+

We work on a monthly retainer with no long-term lock-in contracts. We believe our results should be the reason you stay — not a contract.

How is Solve AI different from hiring a social media manager?

+

A social media manager posts content. We build your entire personal brand system — AI avatar, content strategy, competitor research, script curation, video production and posting — all in one. And because we use AI, you get 20 professional videos a month at a fraction of the cost of a traditional content team.
